Those Who Serve

By Lana Lang  |  Smallville Torch Staff Writer
[ Foto/Ilustração: homenagem ]

Oftentimes we are told to do what's best for ourselves, seek out our dreams, look out for number one. But where would our society be without those who look out for others? How would our nation thrive and succeed without the men and women who serve?

This year, several Smallville families sent sons and daughters into the armed forces, continuing a long tradition in our small town of answering the call when it comes. Their sacrifice rarely makes headlines, but it shapes the freedoms the rest of us often take for granted.

It's not just the soldiers, either. Our local firefighters, police officers, doctors, and teachers all serve in their own way — showing up every day for people who may never know their names. Smallville may be a small dot on the map, but its spirit of service is anything but small.

So the next time you're tempted to chase only your own dreams, remember: greatness isn't just about what you achieve for yourself. Sometimes it's measured by what you're willing to give for others.
